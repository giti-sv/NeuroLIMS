# Neurotransmitters

## 1. Dopamine
**Main Role:** Reward, motivation, movement  
**Simulation Effect:** Can increase activity related to reward and motivation

## 2. Serotonin
**Main Role:** Mood, sleep, emotional balance  
**Simulation Effect:** Can affect mood stability and calmness

## 3. Glutamate
**Main Role:** Main excitatory neurotransmitter  
**Simulation Effect:** Increases neuron activation

## 4. GABA
**Main Role:** Main inhibitory neurotransmitter  
**Simulation Effect:** Reduces neuron activation

## 5. Acetylcholine
**Main Role:** Attention, learning, muscle activation  
**Simulation Effect:** Supports focus and learning signals
